Biography

Lin Zhou is currently an Associate Professor in the School of Cyber Science and Technology at the Beihang University, Beijing, China. Prior to this, he was a Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. From Mar. 2018 to Dec. 2018, he was first a Research Engineer and then a Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the National University of Singapore (NUS). He received the Ph.D. degree in ECE from NUS in 2018 and the B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2014. His research interests include information theory, statistical inference, physical layer security, space-air-ground integrated communications and their applications.
